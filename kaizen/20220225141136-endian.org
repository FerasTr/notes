#+title: Endian

To check for endiannes do the following:

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
echo -n I | od -to2 | head -n1 | cut -f2 -d" " | cut -c6
#+END_SRC

If the result is 0, then the machine is Big-Endian, 1 otherwise.

You can also use
#+BEGIN_SRC sh
lscpu | grep Endian
#+END_SRC
