#+title: VBScript

* Overview
VBScript is an alternative to [[id:91bb374f-27a4-49a0-bb6b-5fb77987abc6][JavaScript]] that is supported only in the Internet Explorer browser. It is modeled on [[id:354c60b7-ffa5-43f4-a949-d8b37fac2540][Visual Basic]] and allows interation with the browser [[id:ca4b97ce-fbfd-44b7-b0ac-3068f280955a][DOM]]
