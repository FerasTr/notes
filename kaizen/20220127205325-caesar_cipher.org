#+title: Caesar Cipher
#+filetags: :math:cryptography:

* Overview
The Caesar Cipher is used by shifting the alphabet in order to encode texts.

ROT13 is the same thing but with a fixed shift of 13 characters.

An example of shifting by 8:
#+begin_example
CAESAR -> KIMAIZ
#+end_example
