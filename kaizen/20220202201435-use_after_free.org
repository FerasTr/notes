#+title: Use After Free
#+filetags: :heap:binaryexp:binary:

* Overview
After calling ~free~ on an allocation, we are free to use that chunk for a future call to ~malloc~. If we forget and then use the freed object, then it might cause unwanted behavior such as crashing or leaking data.
